Prince Joseph’s Fear

Dani Bryant

Prince Joseph from the Kingdom of Fantastic was known across the land for being lionhearted and sincere.

Once upon a time, he decided the time had come to face his fear of heights. He decided to ask his most trusted advisor for some real talk advice. His advisor looked him in the face and said “A fear is like a potato, you must eat it and only then will you be free.”

Joseph did just that! And found that it wasn’t so dreadful after all. Facing his fear was just the beginning of his exciting journey!
