Princess Robin from the Kingdom of Awesome was known across the land for being brave and gutsy.

Once upon a time, she rescued three grumpy princes arguing over their magical inheritance. They were fighting over combat boots with which the wearer could wish themselves anywhere, a key that opened every lock, and an invisibility cape to sneak from place to place.

After a lot of chatting Robin was able to convince the princes to share the objects by trading them every 100 days! For her troubles the Princes granted Robin one time traveling adventure. Robin, chose to go to Peru, where she climbed mountains.
