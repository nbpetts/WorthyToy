Princess Liza’s Joke

Dani Bryant

Princess Liza from the Kingdom of Twee was known across the land for being courageous and savvy.

Once upon a time, she discovered she could talk to trees and animals! And that they could talk back! One cloudy afternoon, she stumbled upon a green monkey who was busy grooving. It seemed very bored so she told it her favorite joke, “Why did the I eat his sword? Because his Mom said it was a piece of cake!

Her new friend just laughed and laughed and gave her a high five. It didn’t know any jokes so they shared a smile and went to find some new adventures!
