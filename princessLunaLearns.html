Princess Luna Learns from History

Dani Bryant

Princess Luna from the Kingdom of Sparkle was known across the land for being plucky and confident.

She knows everything there is to know about famous princesses from history. Her favorite princess role model is Princess Belle. Luna found a podcast about her role model that no one had ever seen or heard before. She then learned that her role model once battled a lonely bull by just listening and being a good friend--while also looking fierce.

This inspired Luna to bond with her Mom and never stop listening!
